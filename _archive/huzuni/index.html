Huzuni is a Minecraft utility mod developed by Hal. Originally a private mod was made by the two entitled ‘Poohbear’, but after a freak accident, it was no longer developed. Thus we’ve moved onto making a new mod. I guess you could say it’s just poohbear renamed, but it really is not. Now, its a free Minecraft mod made to suit your needs when it comes to having an advantage over others.
